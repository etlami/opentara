# Privacy

OpenTara is designed to be fully private and offline.

- **No account.** You never sign in anywhere.
- **No cloud.** The app makes no network requests. Your measurements never leave the device.
- **No analytics or tracking.** No third‑party SDKs.
- **Local storage only.** Profiles and measurements are stored on the device
  (in the app's local storage) and are removed when you delete the app.
- **Bluetooth** is used only to read your scale, on your explicit action.
- **Export is user‑initiated.** CSV/PDF files are created only when you tap export,
  and go wherever *you* choose via the system share sheet.

If a future version adds optional features that leave the device (e.g. iCloud sync or
Apple Health), they will be **opt‑in** and documented here.
